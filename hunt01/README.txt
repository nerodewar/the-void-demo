THE VOID — HUNT 01: ENGINE KILL
Standalone Chapter Two top-down prototype

LAUNCH
Open hunt01/index.html through GitHub Pages, or choose HUNT 01: ENGINE KILL from the main title screen.

MISSION
- Five-minute mission window.
- Restore engine-deck power in the Power Relay Room.
- Prime both manual thrusters in the central Engine Room.
- Lure the black biological organism through either engine-access corridor.
- Align it beneath the port or starboard thruster and activate that chamber's ignition switch.

PLASMA SYSTEM
- The plasma weapon slows, stuns and knocks back the organism.
- It does not kill the organism.
- The weapon holds 12 plasma cells.
- Recharge in the Storage Room. Luna must remain still while the recharge completes.

CONTROLS
Desktop:
- WASD / arrow keys: move
- Mouse: aim
- Left click or Space: fire
- E / Enter: interact
- Q: scanner pulse
- Escape: pause

iPad / touch:
- Left stick: move
- Right stick: aim and fire
- INTERACT: operate terminals, recharge and ignite a thruster
- PULSE: scanner pulse

AUDIO

See assets/sound-effects/README.txt for the complete list.
